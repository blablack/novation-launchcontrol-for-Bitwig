NOVATION – LAUNCHCONTROL

FACTORY TEMPLATES

Press "Factory" and one of the pad to switch between templates:

Page 1 Transport, Volumes & Pans
Page 2 Mutes, Send 1 & 2
Page 3 Records, Macros and Devices
Page 4 Create free slots
USER TEMPLATES

Press "User" and one of the pad to chose switch to one of the user pages. The User-Pages emmit midi events that can be used to midi-learn the knobs and buttons in Bitwig.

NAVIGATION

User the Up and Down arrows to change track page. User the Left and Right arrows to move move left or right in the track list.

TRANSPORT, VOLUMES AND PANS

The top row knobs control track volumes.

The lower row knobs controle track pans.

The pads are mapped to Bitwig transport functions.

1 Stop
2 Play/Pause
3 Arm Record
4 Arm Automation Write
5 Toggle Arrangement Loop
6 Toggle Metronome
7 Toggle Clip Overdub
8 Toggle Arranger Overdub
MUTES, SEND 1 & 2

The top row knobs control send1.

The lower row knobs control send2.

The pads are mapped to mute.

RECORDS, MACROS AND DEVICES

The left 8 knobs are mapped to Macro Functions.

the right 8 knobs are mapped to Device Parameters.

The pads are mapped to record arm.

RECORDS, MACROS AND DEVICES

The knobs aren't currently mapped.

The pads create an empty clip on the next free slot of the selected Track. The pad number is the clip lenght in bars.
