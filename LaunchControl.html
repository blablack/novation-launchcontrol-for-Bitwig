FACTORY TEMPLATES

Transport, Volumes & Pans

USER TEMPLATES

Press "User" and one of the pad to chose switch to one of the user pages. The User-Pages emmit midi events that can be used to midi-learn the knobs and buttons in Bitwig.

TRANSPORT, VOLUMES AND PANS

The left 8 knobs are mapped to Macro Functions.

the right 8 knobs are mapped to Device Parameters.

The pads are mapped to Bitwig transport functions.

1 Stop
2 Play/Pause
3 Arm Record
4 Arm Automation Write
5 Toggle Arrangement Loop
6 Toggle Metronome
7 Toggle Clip Overdub
8 Toggle Arranger Overdub
